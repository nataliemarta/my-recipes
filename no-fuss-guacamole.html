No-Fuss Guacamole

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Juice of 1/2 medium lime
  • One small garlic clove, pressed or minced (or 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: 1-2 tablespoons of fresh, chopped cilantro
  • Serve with: Tortilla chips

Directions

  1. Slice avocado lengthwise all around and twist to open. Scoop out flesh into small bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, garlic, salt, and cilantro (if using).
  3. Mash with potato masher or fork until well combined.
  4. Serve immediately. To store leftovers, press plastic wrap onto surface of guacamole (to prevent browning) and refrigerate.
Picky Eater TipAvocado can be hit or miss with its unique color and texture. Invite a curious spirit and encourage objective feedback ("it's too slippery" instead of "it's yucky").
