Sheet Pan Teriyaki Salmon

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • salt and pepper
  • 1-2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 3/4 cup bottled teriyaki sauce
  • 2-3 cups broccoli florets
  • Serve with: Brown or white rice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cover half of a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
  3. Place salmon fillets skin side down on foil and sprinkle each with salt and pepper. Cover each fillet with about 1 tablespoon of teriyaki sauce, using a spoon to coat each fillet.
  4. Place broccoli florets on the other side of the sheet, add oil, salt, and pepper and toss on the sheet. Place sheet pan in oven.
  5. Put remaining teriyaki sauce (about 1/2 cup) in a small saucepan over low heat. Simmer and stir occasionally. Sauce will thicken into a glaze.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until salmon is done in the center. Brush each fillet with some of the glaze.
  7. Portion rice into bowls and top with one salmon fillet, broccoli, and drizzle with additional glaze.
Picky Eater TipKeep a salmon fillet plain (or serve sauce on the side). Or cut one of the raw fillets into cubes, then coat with teriyaki (or BBQ sauce) and bake on the sheet pan along with the whole fillets.
