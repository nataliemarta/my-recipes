RMN Homemade Yogurt Drink

Serves: 2  ·  Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh red raspberries or strawberries (or thawed frozen berries)
  • 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender and process until smooth.
  2. Divide between two glasses and serve.
Picky Eater TipFeel free to use the fresh or frozen fruit your kids like best, such as blueberries, mango, strawberries, blackberries, or peaches.
