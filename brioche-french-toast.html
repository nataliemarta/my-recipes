RMN Brioche French Toast

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • dash of cinnamon
  • 8 slices Brioche bread (or regular bread)
  • 1/2-1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • syrup + other toppings
  • Serve with: Breakfast meat of your choice like sausage or bacon + fruit smoothies

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, beat eggs, then add milk, vanilla, and a dash of ground cinnamon thoroughly.
  2. Heat a griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (medium high), and lightly coat the surface with oil, butter, or cooking spray.
  3. Dip each bread slice in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides, and place on the griddle. Do not overlap. Work in batches, if needed.
  4. Flip each slice, and cook for 2 minutes more, then remove to a serving dish.
  5. Top with butter, syrup, powdered sugar, or fruit as desired.
Picky Eater TipBrioche is a soft, sweet-tasting bread that should be appealing to most palates. They can enjoy theirs toasted or eaten plain, perhaps with a side of scrambled egg.
