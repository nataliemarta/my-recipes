RMN Shortcut Fish Tacos w/ Mango Salsa

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 box frozen breaded fish sticks or fillets
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 limes
  • 1 garlic clove, minced or pressed
  • Salt
  • 8-12 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 mango
  • 2 T. diced red onion
  • 1 bunch cilantro (optional)
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix (or 1/4 head red cabbage)
  • Serve with: Chips + salsa

Directions

  1. Bake fish sticks or fillets according to package directions.
  2. While oven is preheating for fish, blend together the avocado sauce: Mash the avocado and blend with sour cream, juice from 1/2 lime, garlic, and a pinch of salt until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Char your tortillas around the edges on a gas burner, using tongs to carefully flip them. Or heat them in the microwave or toaster oven. Wrap in a clean kitchen towel and set aside.
  4. For optional mango slaw: Cut mango into small dices, and mix with 2 tablespoons red or white onion, juice of 1/2 lime, a pinch of salt, and cilantro to taste.
  5. Serve fish inside tortillas, topped with avocado sauce, mango salsa and slaw.
Picky Eater TipLet your kids choose their toppings and build their own taco. Or let them eat ingredients separately (ex: reserve some plain mango as slices).
