RMN Roasted Broccoli

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Rinse broccoli florets and pat dry with a paper towel or clean dish towel.
  3. Place broccoli on a baking sheet and drizzle with oil and lemon juice (if using) and sprinkle with salt, using your hands to coat the florets and spread them out in a single layer on the sheet.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until you've reached the desired crispiness.
  5. Remove from oven and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, stirring to coat. Serve.
