Skillet Chicken Parmesan

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 thin-sliced boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pound dry pasta, any shape
  • Serve with: Pasta and green salads, roasted broccoli, or roasted Brussels sprouts

Directions

  1. Sprinkle thin-sliced chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Dip chicken into beaten egg and then dredge in breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat. When hot, add chicken and cook each side 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.
  4. Set a pot of generously salted water to boil for pasta.
  5. Top each cutlet with a generous spoonful of sauce, a slice of mozzarella, and a sprinkle of Italian seasoning. Bake at 400 degrees F for about 10-12 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  6. While chicken is cooking, cook pasta according to package directions.
  7. Serve chicken alongside pasta and more sauce.
  8. Note: If you don't have an oven-safe skillet, transfer chicken to a baking dish to bake.
Picky Eater TipHold the toppings and cook a plain breaded chicken breast if that's what they prefer. Slicing the cooked cutlets into strips may offer a more familiar and less overwhelming shape and size. Cut across the grain for more tender bites.
