RMN Slow Cooker Beef & Noodles

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 6-8 hours

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1.5-2 pounds beef stew meat
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Parmesan cheese and dried parsley (optional, for topping)
  • Serve with: Roasted broccoli

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine flour and all spices (salt-paprika). Add stew meat and toss to coat pieces.
  2. Add oil to a skillet and set over medium-high heat. Add the flour-coated meat and brown pieces well (they won't be cooked through, just browned).
  3. Scrape contents of pan into the slow cooker. Add beef broth and stir to combine.
  4. Set slow cooker to LOW and cook for 6-8 hours until meat is very tender.
  5. When beef is ready, combine cornstarch and 2 tablespoons water in a small dish and mix well. Stir cornstarch slurry into slow cooker mixture and stir while continuing to cook, until the mixture is thickened a bit.
  6. Turn off heat on slow cooker and stir in sour cream.
  7. Serve over cooked egg noodles and top with Parmesan cheese and dried parsley if desired.
Picky Eater TipReserve a couple pieces of extra lean meat and fry them in a skillet, without the flavorful sauce. Serve with plain egg noodles and Parmesan cheese.
