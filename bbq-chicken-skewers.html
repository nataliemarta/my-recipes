RMN BBQ Chicken Skewers

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds chicken tenders, about 10 tenders
  • 4 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Serve with: Broiled asparagus, baked potatoes

Directions

  1. Thread chicken tenders onto 10 skewers. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water 20-30 minutes before grilling to prevent scorching.
  2. Combine brown sugar, paprika, salt, and black pepper in a bowl; stir well, and rub over tenderloins.
  3. Place skewers on a broiler pan coated with cooking spray; broil 8 minutes on each side or until done, turning skewers occasionally.
Picky Eater TipTake care with sharp skewers (little ones may be intimidated)--remove the meat and chop into pieces, serve with a toothpick if they feel like they're missing out. Some kids prefer to dip plain chicken in sauce on the side versus a baked-on glaze.
