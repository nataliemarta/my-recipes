RMN Tuna (or Chicken) Noodle Casserole

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups dry egg noodles (6 oz)
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 pinch nutmeg
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 5-ounce cans tuna or 1 1/2 cups chopped or shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Serve with: Green salads

Directions

  1. Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish with spray or butter and preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large saucepan, cook egg noodles.
  3. Place frozen peas in a colander in the sink. When egg noodles are finished (about 4 minutes or until tender), drain them in the colander on top of the peas.
  4. While the noodles cook, melt butter over medium heat in another medium saucepan. Whisk in flour until smooth, then stir in seasonings. Slowly whisk in milk and continue cooking, stirring frequently (don't let it stick to the bottom!). Let it come to a boil, then reduce heat and cook another few minutes until thick. Remove from heat.
  5. Add cooked noodles and veggies from colander to the pan with cream sauce, plus tuna or chicken, and stir everything together well. Pour into greased baking dish. Top with breadcrumbs and cheese. Bake 10-15 minutes or until bubbly around edges and crispy on top.
Picky Eater TipReserve some of the cooked noodles and let your kids build a "tuna bowl" with the veggies and cheese.
