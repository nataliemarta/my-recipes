RMN Main Dish Salads

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ALDI Red Bag breaded chicken breasts (look in the freezer aisle)
  • 1 pouch 90-Second Quinoa & Brown rice mix (heat-and-serve rice pouch)
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon grainy mustard or Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch each salt and pepper
  • 1 red apple
  • 6-8 cups mixed greens (I like the ALDI Artisan Lettuces)
  • 1/2 cup walnuts or sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup feta or blue cheese
  • Serve with: Crusty bread or garlic bread

Directions

  1. Bake chicken according to package directions.
  2. While chicken is baking, heat the quinoa/rice pouch according to directions and set aside to cool.
  3. In a small jar, combine vinegar, maple syrup, mustard, olive oil, salt, and pepper and shake to mix.
  4. Chop apple.
  5. To each individual bowl, add a bed of mixed greens, top with 1/4 of the quinoa/rice mixture, 1/4 of the chopped apple, 2 tablespoons of nuts, and 1 tablespoon of soft cheese.
  6. Slice baked chicken and place 1 sliced breast on top of each salad. Drizzle with dressing.
Picky Eater TipLet go of any preconceived notions of reasonable salad composition, and let your kids build their salads using what they like best.
