RMN Tortellini With Creamy Lemon Sauce

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1-2 packages cheese tortellini (4 servings total)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup pasta water
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1/2 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • Large handful baby spinach, chopped
  • Parmesan for topping
  • Serve with: Baguette or garlic bread + green salads

Directions

  1. Cook tortellini according to package directions and set aside 1 cup pasta water (the easiest way is to drain tortellini into a glass measuring cup).
  2. In skillet, melt butter, add garlic and stir 30 seconds to cook. Add flour and stir.
  3. Add milk and the 1 cup cooking water, stirring constantly with a whisk while cooking for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add cream cheese and melt in. Add lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and pepper and stir.
  5. Add cooked tortellini and chopped spinach and toss everything to coat.
  6. Spoon into bowls and top with Parmesan cheese if desired.
Picky Eater TipKeep some of the tortellini plain and serve the sauce on the side or serve with butter and parmesan cheese.
