RMN Biscuit Pot Pie

Serves: 4-6  ·  Total Time: Prep 35 min, Cook 20-25 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup carrot, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup celery, finely diced
  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup half-and-half
  • 1 can refrigerated biscuits
  • 1 egg
  • Serve with: Green salads

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In medium saucepan, boil carrots, celery, and chicken breasts. When chicken is cooked and veggies are soft, drain and shred chicken breasts. Set aside.
  3. In an oven-proof skillet melt butter and cook onion until softened. Add flour and seasonings (thyme through pepper) and stir until combined.
  4. Add chicken broth and half-and-half and cook on medium, stirring frequently, until thick.
  5. Stir in the carrots, celery, and shredded chicken. Top with biscuits (if biscuits are jumbo-sized, cut carefully in half crosswise with a serrated knife).
  6. In a small dish, beat the egg and brush tops of biscuits with it.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the tops of the biscuits are a deep golden brown.
  8. Note: If you don't have an oven-proof skillet, use whatever skillet you have then transfer the mixture into a 9x13" pan before baking.
Picky Eater TipReserve some of the plain shredded chicken and bake one biscuit plain. Let your child make a biscuit sandwich with the chicken or add a little bit of the gravy.
