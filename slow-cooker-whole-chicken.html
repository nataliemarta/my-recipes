Slow Cooker Whole Chicken

Serves: 6  ·  Total Time: About 4 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 small yellow onions
  • 1 4-5 lb whole chicken, giblets removed
  • Serve with: Mashed potatoes and sauteed green beans

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mix seasonings together (brown sugar through pepper). Add oil and stir.
  2. Chop onions and place half on bottom of slow cooker insert. Place rest of onions inside chicken cavity.
  3. Crumple 3 pieces of aluminum foil into balls and place on top of onions. Place chicken onto foil balls, breast side up.
  4. Spread seasoning/oil mixture all over chicken. Pull up skin and spread underneath if you can.
  5. Cover and cook on HIGH for 4-5 hours.
  6. When finished, transfer chicken carefully to a sheet pan. Heat broiler to HIGH and place under broiler for 5-10 minutes until skin is slightly crispy and brown.
  7. Remove to cutting board, let it rest for 5-10 minutes, then cut into pieces and serve.
  8. Note: All slow cookers are different, so check temperature with a meat thermometer. It should register at least 165 degrees.
Picky Eater TipKids may enjoy the drumstick, which they can hold in their hands. Or serve some of the meat (without skin) with a favorite sauce like ketchup or BBQ on the side and let them dip.
