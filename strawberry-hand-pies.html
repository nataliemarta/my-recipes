Strawberry Hand Pies

Makes 12 hand pies  ·  Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 refrigerated pie crusts (one box)
  • 1 1/2 cups diced or thinly sliced strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (plus more for sprinkling on top)
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 egg, beaten

Directions

  1. Remove pie crusts from refrigerator and leave on countertop according to package directions (makes it easier to roll).
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Unroll each crust on a lightly floured surface (or on silicone baking mat, no flour needed). Roll into slightly bigger circles w/ rolling pin.
  4. Cut into 3-inch rounds with biscuit cutter or top of glass. Repeat with second crust, re-rolling scraps until you get about 24 circles. Make 4 slits in half of the circles; those are the tops. Cover the 24 circles with damp, clean dish towel.
  5. Slice strawberries into dices or thin slices, toss with sugar, flour, and lemon juice.
  6. On half of the circles, place a small pile of berries in the center.
  7. Beat egg and brush around the edges of the bottom crusts. Place top crusts on. Holding top crust in place with one hand, use other hand to press fork around edges to seal.
  8. Brush tops of pies with beaten egg and sprinkle with sugar.
  9. Bake about 15 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. Cool slightly before serving (they'll be hot inside!).
Picky Eater TipFeel free to use any other fruit such as blueberries, peaches, cherries, or apples. Or try hazelnut spread (you won't need to cut vents in the tops, but poke a few holes in the tops with a fork before baking instead).
