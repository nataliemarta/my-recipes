Cozy Sausage & Spinach Pasta

Serves: 8  ·  Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry rigatoni pasta
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, ground
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 six-ounce can tomato paste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2.5 ounces fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped (I use half of a five-ounce bag)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Serve with: Green salads

Directions

  1. Set a pot of well-salted water over medium-high heat, then cook pasta according to package directions, reserving about a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While water is coming to a boil, heat oil in large skillet. Add sausage and cook, breaking it up into bite-sized pieces.
  3. When sausage is almost done, add minced garlic and stir to cook. Add tomato paste and broth and stir until combined.
  4. Turn heat to low. Add heavy cream and chopped spinach and stir.
  5. Add cooked pasta and stir, drizzling in enough pasta water to thin the sauce a bit.
  6. Taste and add salt and pepper if needed.
  7. Portion into bowls. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.
Picky Eater TipLeave out the spinach (or make a small portion without it) if that's a sticking point. Plain noodles are okay too, especially if you have any fruit or milk on hand to balance out the plate!
