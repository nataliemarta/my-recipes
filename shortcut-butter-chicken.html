Shortcut Butter Chicken

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breasts
  • 1 sweet bell pepper (any color)
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive or canola oil
  • 1 jar Burman's Butter Chicken Simmer Sauce
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • Serve with: Brown basmati rice, roasted broccoli, and warm Naan bread

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Sprinkle all over with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Dice or chop the pepper and onion (however big or small you like).
  3. Heat the oil in a large skillet. When hot, add the onion and pepper and saute for a few minutes until soft.
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until lightly browned.
  5. Pour the jar of Butter Chicken Simmer Sauce over everything, cover, and simmer for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in the yogurt. Taste and add salt and pepper as needed.
Picky Eater TipLeave out onion and pepper (or dice them very finely) or cook some of the diced chicken in a separate skillet without the simmer sauce (offer it on the side as a dip).
