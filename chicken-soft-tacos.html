RMN Chicken Soft Tacos

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4-1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Splash of white vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 limes
  • 1 garlic clove, pressed or minced
  • Drizzle of olive or canola oil
  • 1 to 1.5 pounds chicken tenderloins
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8-10 small, taco-sized tortillas
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Serve with: Chips + salsa, fresh fruit

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine sliced red onion with a splash of vinegar, and a pinch each of salt and sugar. Set aside.
  2. In another small bowl, stir together sour cream, zest and juice of one lime, garlic, and a pinch of salt. Set aside. Cut second lime into quarters and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat a drizzle of oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Sprinkle chicken tenders all over with taco seasoning and add to hot skillet, cooking on both sides until 165 degrees F inside.
  5. Turn off heat and remove chicken to a cutting board. Shred with two forks. Return shredded chicken to the skillet and toss to get chicken completely covered with seasoning.
  6. Heat tortillas on a gas burner or wrap in a moist paper towel and microwave until warm.
  7. Fill warm tortillas with shredded chicken and top with diced avocado, pickled onions, and a drizzle of the limey, garlicky sour cream.
  8. Serve with lime wedges.
Picky Eater TipKeep some chicken plain if they don't like seasoning. Let kids build their own taco. Offer cheese or other favorite toppings as choices too.
