BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 package chicken drumsticks (about 10 drumsticks)
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 to 1 cup barbecue sauce (bottled or homemade)
  • Serve with: Boiled and buttered baby potatoes, corn on the cob, and salads

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, stir together seasonings (garlic powder through salt) and oil.
  2. Add drumsticks to the bowl and mix well, coating all of the pieces. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes but up to 8 hours.
  3. When ready to make dinner, preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  4. Take drumsticks out of the bowl and place on a foil-covered baking sheet, making sure not to overlap them.
  5. Place in preheated oven and bake for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove baking sheet from oven, brush each drumstick with barbecue sauce, flip, and brush again. Put chicken back in the oven to bake an additional 20 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and brush with more barbecue sauce.
  8. To grill: Place drumsticks on a grill set to medium-high heat. Cook for about 30 minutes, flipping drumsticks every 5-10 minutes. Brush with BBQ sauce halfway through cooking, then again at end.
Picky Eater TipLeave a drumstick plain and let them choose their dip/sauce. Or remove the cooked meat from the drumstick to make it easier to eat.
