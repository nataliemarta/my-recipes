RMN Spaghetti Carbonara

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese, plus more for garnishing
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 strips uncooked bacon, chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • 12 oz. spaghetti noodles
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh or dried parsley
  • 1-2 pinches kosher salt
  • Serve with: Green salads and garlic bread or Italian bread

Directions

  1. Mix Parmesan cheese and eggs together in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Set a large pot of water to boil.
  3. Heat olive oil in a deep skillet or Dutch oven and add bacon, stirring frequently.
  4. While bacon is cooking, add spaghetti to boiling water.
  5. Fry bacon pieces until crisp. Add garlic and cook together for about 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Turn heat to low.
  6. Drain the spaghetti, reserving 1 cup of pasta water. Immediately pour drained noodles into the pan with the bacon, tossing well to coat.
  7. Remove pan from heat. Add the cheese/egg mixture slowly to the spaghetti while tossing. The hot pasta will cook the eggs (but don't let them scramble!).
  8. Stir in some of the pasta water, a small amount at a time, for extra creaminess, until you get a texture you like.
  9. Add parsley and 1-2 big pinches of salt and toss again to coat.
  10. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley and more Parmesan.
Picky Eater TipReserve some plain pasta, plus a few pieces of cooked bacon. Let kids top their pasta with bacon and Parmesan.
