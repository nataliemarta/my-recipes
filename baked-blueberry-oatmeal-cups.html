Baked Blueberry Oatmeal Cups

Makes 1 dozen  ·  Total Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 3 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 3 small ripe bananas, peeled
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons white chocolate chips (to taste)

Directions

  1. Measure out the blueberries and set aside to defrost slightly.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Line a muffin tin with liners.
  3. Place oats in a food processor or blender and process for 10 seconds until some of the oats are finely ground (some will still be in larger pieces).
  4. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas well. Add the oats, milk, honey, baking powder, vanilla, and salt and stir well to combine.
  5. Fold in the white chocolate chips, then gently fold in the blueberries. The mixture will be wet, not thick like muffin batter.
  6. Spoon the mixture into the liners, which will be very full. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until tops are browned and mixture looks completely set.
