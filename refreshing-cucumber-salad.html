Refreshing Cucumber Salad

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium cucumber, peeled and sliced
  • 1-2 tablespoons diced red onion (can also use white)
  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar or seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • Big pinch each salt & pepper
  • Serve with: Anything as a side salad!

Directions

  1. Place sliced cucumbers and diced onion in a small bowl.
  2. Add vinegar and water and toss to coat.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Let them sit a few minutes for the flavor to build while you're prepping other things. Then serve and enjoy.
Picky Eater TipIf someone in your house doesn't like raw onion, try grating a little bit of it to avoid big pieces. Dried onion powder can work this magic as well. Or serve cucumber slices plain and raw, with their choice of dip, instead.
