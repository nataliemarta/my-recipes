RMN Slow Cooker 2-Packet Pot Roast

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 8 hours 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 packet Italian dressing mix
  • 1 packet Ranch dressing mix
  • One 2-3 lb beef chuck roast
  • 2-3 large carrots, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 large russet potatoes, cut into 2-inch chunks
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional, for gravy)
  • 2 tablespoons flour (optional, for gravy)

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk both seasoning packets into a cup of beef broth.
  2. Place roast into slow cooker insert and pour broth mixture over top.
  3. Arrange cut potatoes and carrots around the meat and cook on low for 8 hours.
  4. Remove cooked meat to a serving platter.
  5. For optional gravy: Strain a few tablespoons of drippings and add to a saucepan along with a tablespoon of butter. Stir in 2 tablespoons of flour and cook until combined. Whisk in a cup of beef broth and cook over medium heat until thickened.
Picky Eater TipRoast the veggies separately, mash the potatoes instead, or serve a side of raw carrots if preferred. Shredding the beef and serving a little on the bread as a sandwich can help them get familiar with these flavors as well.
