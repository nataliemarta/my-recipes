RMN Twice-Baked Potatoes

Serves: 4-6  ·  Total Time: 1 hour, 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 russet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cup small curd cottage cheese
  • 1 heaping cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen, loose leaf chopped spinach (or 1 cup frozen broccoli, diced small)
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 4 tablespoons bacon bits (optional)
  • Serve with: Green salads or ALDI red bag chicken for a bigger meal

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Scrub the potatoes and pat dry. Poke all over with a fork, then rub with oil. Place on a baking tray and salt generously on both sides.
  3. Bake for 1 hour. Potatoes are done when a knife easily pierces the center (or 210F).
  4. Meanwhile, steam spinach or broccoli and pat dry. Mix with cottage cheese, shredded cheese, two generous pinches of salt, and one pinch of pepper in a medium bowl.
  5. When potatoes are fully baked, carefully cut each one in half, lengthwise. Scoop out most of the flesh with a spoon, leaving a thin layer inside potato skin.
  6. Add scooped potato flesh to the cheese mixture and mash well with a fork or potato masher. Add enough milk to make the filling fluffy but not wet.
  7. Spoon mashed potato mixture into skins (will be heaping). Top with bacon bits and some extra cheese. Bake for 10 more minutes.
Picky Eater TipLet your kid customize their own baked potato--eaten plain, with butter, cheese, or whatever they like best.
