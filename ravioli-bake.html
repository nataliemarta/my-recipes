RMN Ravioli Bake

Serves: 6  ·  Total Time: 1 hour 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen cheese (or beef) ravioli
  • 1 jar pasta sauce, 32 oz
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Serve with: Green salads

Directions

  1. Set your oven to 350 degrees F and prepare a 9x13-inch baking dish by lightly spraying the bottom and sides with cooking spray.
  2. Spread a scant cup of pasta sauce all over the bottom of the dish, add the frozen ravioli, and top with the rest of the sauce, shaking or stirring gently to make sure all the pasta is coated.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella on top.
  4. Cover with foil and bake 40 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 minutes more or until bubbly and very hot in the center.
  5. Let stand for about 10 minutes before serving.
Picky Eater TipSet aside a few ravioli to boil plain or use a little bit of other dry pasta from your pantry to serve with (or without) the sauce.
