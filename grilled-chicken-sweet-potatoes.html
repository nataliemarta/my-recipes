RMN Grilled Chicken & Sweet Potatoes

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 3 hours, 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup plain yogurt (Greek or regular)
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1.5-2 pounds chicken breasts (at least 4 breasts, thin-sliced or pounded thin)
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes (can use white instead)
  • Serve with: Grilled asparagus or zucchini

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil, Italian seasoning, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Add chicken breasts, turning to coat. Cover bowl and refrigerate at least 3 hours.
  2. Peel and slice sweet potato into 1/4-inch thick rounds and toss in remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  3. Spray grill with cooking spray and heat to medium-high heat. Place sweet potatoes and chicken on the grill. Grill sweet potatoes for 4 minutes on each side. Cook chicken 5 minutes on each side or until done.
  4. Remove chicken and sweet potatoes to a platter. Sprinkle sweet potatoes with salt. Serve.
Picky Eater TipKids may want to dip their potatoes in ketchup or BBQ sauce. Pairing unfamiliar or new flavors with something comfortable is a great way to slowly expand preferences.
