RMN Flatbread Pizza

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 package flatbread (2 crusts total)
  • Pizza sauce, amount to preference
  • Shredded or fresh mozzarella (I like a combo of both), amount to preference
  • 1/2 package pepperoni, finely chopped
  • Other toppings of choice (like mushrooms, peppers, etc.)
  • Oregano or Italian seasoning
  • 1-2 tablespoons balsamic glaze (optional--look for this at ALDI with the vinegar)
  • Serve with: Green salads or fruit

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Bake crusts for 5-7 minutes or until lightly browned.
  2. Top flatbreads with pizza sauce, cheese, pepperoni, and other toppings of your choice. Sprinkle oregano or Italian seasoning on top.
  3. Return to oven and bake an additional 5-7 minutes or until toppings are melty and hot.
  4. Slice into strips or wedges, drizzle lightly with balsamic glaze (a little goes a long way!), and serve.
  5. Note: You can bake the crusts on a baking sheet, on a pizza stone, or directly on the oven rack. They will cook more quickly on the oven rack or pizza stone, so reduce time and check bottom for browning. You can also grill these.
Picky Eater TipLet your kids build their own flatbread crust (or half of flatbread).
