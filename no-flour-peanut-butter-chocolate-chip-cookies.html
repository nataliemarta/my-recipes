RMN No-Flour Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Makes about 24 cookies  ·  Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup oats (quick oats work best)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 2-3 tablespoons white sugar (for rolling)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix all ingredients together in a medium bowl.
  3. Roll into 1-inch balls or use rounded teaspoons (I use a cookie scoop).
  4. Put white sugar in a small bowl and coat the balls with sugar before placing onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  5. Gently press the tops of the cookies with the back of a fork twice to make a crisscross design.
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes or just until the tops start to look dry. They may look underbaked, but they will harden as they cool (but stay soft and chewy).
  7. Cool 1-2 minutes, then remove from baking sheet and place on cooling rack.
