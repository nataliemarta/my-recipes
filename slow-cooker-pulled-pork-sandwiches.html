RMN Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Serves: 10  ·  Total Time: Hands-on 10 min, Cook 8 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 pound pork shoulder or butt
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 bottle BBQ sauce
  • 4-6 sandwich-size buns (or 1 package sliders)
  • Optional for slaw: 1 package coleslaw mix, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Serve with: Cole slaw, mac-n-cheese, corn on cob

Directions

  1. Combine garlic powder, paprika, chili powder, and salt and rub all over pork.
  2. Place pork in the slow cooker insert. Combine 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar and 1/2 cup BBQ sauce and pour over pork.
  3. Cover and cook for 8 hours on LOW.
  4. Remove pork from the slow cooker and shred into pieces, removing any fat or bone.
  5. Drain all liquid from slow cooker except about 1/2 cup. Return pork to slow cooker, switch to "warm" setting, and gently stir in more BBQ sauce to taste.
  6. Optional: Toss coleslaw mix with Greek yogurt, cider vinegar, and sugar.
  7. Serve on buns with additional BBQ sauce and top with coleslaw if desired.
Picky Eater TipKids may enjoy smaller slider buns. You can also leave a portion of the cooked pork plain and serve BBQ sauce on the side.
