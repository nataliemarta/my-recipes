Charcuterie Dinner Board

Serves: 4-6  ·  Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 choices cheese, such as brie, cheddar, and goat
  • 2 choices meat, such as salami, prosciutto, or deli turkey
  • 2 choices veggies, such as baby carrots, snap peas, cherry tomatoes, or cucumbers
  • 2 choices fresh fruit, such as grapes, sliced apples, or berries
  • 1 baguette, sliced thin into rounds
  • 1 box crackers of your choice
  • 1 package dried fruit, such as apricots, mango, or dates
  • Optional: hummus, jam, pesto, nuts, olives, pickles

Directions

  1. Arrange everything on a large cutting board, baking sheet, or tray.
  2. Let everyone dig in!
Picky Eater TipThis is the ultimate picky eater plate! Let your kids help you select items for the board and choose which ones they want to eat.
