RMN Sheet Pan Chicken Sausage & Veggies

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed sweet potato
  • 4 links chicken sausage
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon + 3 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon mustard (Dijon or stoneground)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Serve with: Bread or garlic bread

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Spray a sheet pan with cooking spray and set aside.
  2. Cut sweet potato, broccoli, and peppers into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a medium bowl, toss sweet potato with 1 teaspoon of olive oil and place on baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes.
  4. While sweet potato bakes, whisk sauce ingredients together in a small dish: maple syrup, 1 tablespoon olive oil, mustard, garlic, thyme, and salt.
  5. In the same bowl you tossed the potatoes in, toss sausage, broccoli, and pepper in 2 teaspoons olive oil. Place on baking sheet with potatoes, brush with half the sauce, and bake everything for 15 additional minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, brush with remaining sauce, and serve.
Picky Eater TipLet kids choose what they want from the sheet pan. Keep a small section without sauce. Building a little toothpick skewer with tiny bites might encourage reluctant eaters to try things together.
