RMN Shortcut Meatball Subs

Serves: 6  ·  Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon + 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 1 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 package frozen meatballs (1/2 ounce size)
  • 6-8 sub, brat, or hot dog buns
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Dried or fresh parsley, optional, for garnish
  • Serve with: Green salads or other veggie side

Directions

  1. To make the meatball sub sauce: In a medium skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil on medium heat for 15 seconds, then add garlic, oregano, and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 minute. Add crushed tomatoes and stir well; add salt and sugar and stir again until combined.
  2. Add entire package of meatballs to sauce, stirring so meatballs are covered in sauce.
  3. Cover pan and cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Move oven rack to top position, and heat the broiler to HIGH.
  5. Spoon saucy meatballs into buns, top with cheese, and place on a baking sheet under the broiler for 1-2 minutes, checking frequently to make sure they don't burn.
Picky Eater TipHot meatballs can be a big mouthful. Chop them into halves or quarters and cut the sandwich in half or thirds to make easy sliders. Or serve crushed meatballs in sauce with toasted buns to use as "breadsticks" for dipping.
