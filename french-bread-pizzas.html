French Bread Pizzas

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 1 jar spaghetti or pizza sauce (or pesto)
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese (or 1 bag shredded mozzarella)
  • 1 bag pepperoni (or other toppings your family likes)
  • Serve with: Green salads or fruit

Directions

  1. Move oven rack to position closest to the broiler. Cover a sheet pan with foil or parchment and turn broiler to HIGH.
  2. Cut loaf in half, then slice the halves open horizontally and toast under the broiler 2-3 minutes or until slightly crisp (watch it closely so it doesn't burn!).
  3. Spread sauce or pesto onto the cut sides, top with cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  4. Broil for 3-5 minutes more until they're toasty and melty.
  5. Cool 5 minutes, then cut into slices and serve.
Picky Eater TipLet your kids choose their toppings and build their own pizzas. Or broil a piece with only cheese and let them dip it into the sauce like a breadstick.
