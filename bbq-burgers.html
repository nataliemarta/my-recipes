BBQ Burgers

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: About 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (this makes 4 burgers; use 1.5 pounds if you want 6 burgers)
  • 3 tablespoons barbecue sauce (bottled or homemade)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 slices cheese of choice
  • 4-6 hamburger buns
  • Serve with: Watermelon wedges, corn on the cob

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except olive oil.
  2. Divide meat mixture into four sections and form each into a ball, then into patties.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet (or preheat the grill to medium) and cook burgers about 5 minutes each side or until they reach 160 degrees F internal temperature. (Top with cheese in the last few minutes of cooking if you'd like.)
  4. Let burgers sit for 5 minutes while you toast or grill the buns.
Picky Eater TipIf your kids don't like spices and seasonings yet, feel free to make a plain burger patty for them with just salt.
