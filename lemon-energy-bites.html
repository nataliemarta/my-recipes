RMN Lemon Energy Bites

Makes 12-14 bites  ·  Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large lemon
  • 1 cup oats (old-fashioned or quick)
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 7 dates (pits removed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut

Directions

  1. Zest lemon, then juice it into the same dish.
  2. Place oats, walnuts, dates, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla in a food processor.
  3. Blend until it becomes a sticky dough.
  4. Roll into balls (about 1 tablespoon each), then roll in shredded coconut.
  5. Store in the refrigerator in an air-tight container.
Picky Eater TipSkip the coconut coating if your kid isn't a fan (or blend it into the dough instead of using it as a coating).
