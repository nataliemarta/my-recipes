RMN Grilled Ranch Chicken Kebabs

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1.5 pounds chicken breasts
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 package whole button mushrooms
  • Serve with: Corn on the cob and watermelon or other fruit

Directions

  1. Whisk together the marinade: olive oil through black pepper.
  2. Cut chicken into one-inch pieces and cover with the marinade. Leave refrigerated at least an hour or two, or all day if you'd like.
  3. Cut peppers into one-inch pieces.
  4. Soak wooden skewers in water for about 20-30 minutes to help prevent burning, then thread the chicken, peppers, and mushrooms onto the skewers.
  5. Coat the grill with cooking spray and preheat to medium.
  6. Grill skewers 4-5 minutes on each side, brushing with reserved marinade while cooking. The kebabs are ready when the chicken has reached an internal temperature of 165 degrees F.
Picky Eater TipRemove meat from the skewer before serving. Mushrooms and peppers can impart a strong flavor (and smell) so thread a couple kabobs with just chicken - leaving space between the pieces so they cook thoroughly.
