Jammy Pork Chops

Serves: 4  ·  Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless center cut pork chops (about 1 lb)
  • 1 tablespoon oil such as olive or canola
  • 1/4 cup strawberry jam
  • 2 tablespoons brown or Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Serve with: Buttered egg noodles and sauteed green beans

Directions

  1. Pat chops dry on both sides and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add pork chops and cook until browned, 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. While chops are cooking, mix jam, mustard, vinegar, and soy sauce in a small bowl.
  4. Pour sauce over chops and simmer an additional 5-8 minutes or until pork is done and flavors meld, turning occasionally (145 degrees F on a meat thermometer).
  5. Note: This recipe works well with any flavor jam, preserves, or jelly.
Picky Eater TipLet your kids pick the jam flavor. Remove one of the smaller pork chops when fully cooked before simmering it in the sauce if you plan to serve sauce on the side.
